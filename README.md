# 🧠 AI-Powered Tax Optimizer

Welcome to the **AI-Powered Tax Optimizer** – a smart and simple tool that helps you estimate your taxes and get personalized suggestions to save more through smart deductions and investments.

Built with 💡 machine learning and 🖥️ Streamlit, this app gives users a clear and friendly way to understand their annual tax situation — and find smart ways to reduce it.

---

## ✨ What It Does

- ✅ **Estimates Annual Tax**: Based on your income sources and deductions.
- 📘 **Recommends Deductions**: Personalized tips to maximize your tax savings.
- 💰 **Investment Advice**: Suggestions based on patterns from similar financial profiles.
- 📊 **Clean UI**: Input your values and get instant results with an easy-to-use interface.
- 📈 **Visual Insights**: Includes a correlation heatmap from data exploration.

---

## 📦 Tech Stack

- **Streamlit** – for building the interactive web app  
- **Scikit-learn** – for machine learning models  
- **Pandas & NumPy** – for data processing  
- **Joblib** – for loading trained ML models
